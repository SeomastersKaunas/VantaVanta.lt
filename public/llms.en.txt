## Quick FAQ

- **What is a "vanta"?** A traditional Lithuanian sauna whisk – a tied bundle of tree branches used to gently brush and tap the body in a hot sauna to improve circulation, open pores, and release leaf aroma.
- **Birch, European oak, or Canadian oak – which to pick?** Birch: classic, soft, aromatic, ideal for first-timers and frequent users. European oak: firmer, better at moving steam and heat. Canadian oak (Quercus rubra): the strongest character.
- **Single piece or wholesale only?** Both. Single retail by phone; wholesale tiers from 50 pcs via the on-site form.
- **Where are they made?** Lithuania – branches selected from forests, hand-tied by experienced makers, naturally dried.
